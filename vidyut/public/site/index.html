  • Technology & Global Consulting - Companies establishing or strengthening their CSR function, including those with India-specific mandates under the Companies Act.
  • Corporate Foundations - Companies looking to register, restructure, or formalise grantmaking mechanisms with proper legal and governance frameworks.
  • HR & Leadership Teams - Teams seeking to establish or strengthen DEI, POSH compliance, and employee volunteering programmes connected to business values.
  • Start-ups & MNCs in India - Newly mandated companies that need to build the CSR function from scratch with rigorous strategy and governance.

FIT · WHEN

Situations that bring me in

Organisations reach out when they face a clear gap between stated intent and operational reality.

  • CSR spend exists but there is no coherent strategy, governance, or multi-year roadmap behind it.
  • Grant programs are running but lack rigorous due diligence, impact monitoring, or compliance processes.
  • A corporate foundation needs to be registered or restructured with proper legal and governance frameworks.
  • The organisation needs to respond to a disaster but has no corporate response protocol in place.
  • A company is newly mandated under the Companies Act ond needs to build the function from scratch.
  • Employee volunteering is low-engagement and disconnected from business values.
  • DEI commitments are stated on paper but ERGs, POSH compliance, and inclusion practices are absent or unstructured.
  • Internal CSR communication is fragmented and leadership visibility into impact is limited.
